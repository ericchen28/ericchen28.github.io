Bio

I am a first year PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University. Previously, I received my bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Statistics from Columbia University. I am broadly interested in the intersection of causal inference, data-driven decision-making, explainable AI, and uncertainty quantification. Specifically, I hope to develop effective, reliable, and scalable tools for real-world problems that build on mathematical insights.
